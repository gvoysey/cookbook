Vegetable Chili

1 med. size eggplant, unpeeled, cut into 1/2 in. cubes
3/4 c. olive oil
2 med. size yellow onions, diced
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped or minced
2 lg. green bell peppers, cored, seeded, and diced
2 cans Italian plum tomatoes, cut into chunks (can just break them up while cooking instead)
2 T. chili powder
2 tsp. ground cumin
1 T. dried oregano
1 T. dried basil
2 tsp. freshly ground pepper
1 tsp. salt
1/2 c. chopped Italian parsley (used dried parsley)
1 (16-oz) can kidney beans, drained
1 (16-oz) can chickpeas, drained
2 T. fresh lemon juice (can use bottled)

Place 1/2 cup olive oil and eggplant in a large pot.  Saute until eggplant is just tender.  Remove eggplant from pan and set aside.  Add remaining oil and onions, garlic, and peppers to the pot.  Saute until tender, about 10 minutes.  Add tomatoes, spices, and parsley.  Simmer about 30 minutes.  Add beans and lemon juice.  Return eggplant.  Simmer about 15 minutes or until beans and eggplant are tender.  Serve over brown rice with shredded cheddar cheese.
