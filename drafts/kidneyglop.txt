kidney bean based glop
(ian and vee)

1 lb. dry kidney beans
3 bell peppers
1 onion
1 potato
3 cloves garlic
1 lb. 80/20 ground beef
season to taste: salt, pepper, chili flakes, chicken boullion powder, dried
basil

soak kidney beans overnight
brown beef with salt and pepper to taste
dice peppers, onion, potato
mince garlic
put everything in a crock pot with about two cups of water
put on low heat for ten hours
wait
eat!

serving suggestions: over rice, with cheddar and frozen spinach (defrosted, of
course)

refridgerate for about a week; put in jars to bring to work for microwave
lunches.

freezes well.
